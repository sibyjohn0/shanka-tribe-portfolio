About Shanka Tribe

Hailing from Kerala, Shanka Tribe creates an immersive blend of organic trance and world music, channeling the pulse of ancestral rhythms and the spirit of unity. Their sound weaves together chants, tribal instruments, and global grooves to evoke deep emotion and movement.

Each performance begins with the blowing of the shankh - a ritual of awakening and purification. Shanka Tribe invites listeners into a journey of rhythm, connection, and inner elevation.

Looking ahead, Shanka Tribe envisions a global movement that bridges cultures through music, creating transformative experiences that unite communities and elevate consciousness. Their mission is to expand the reach of organic world music while maintaining the spiritual essence and authenticity that defines their sound.

UMOJA

The New EP

Unity. Connection. Hope.

Umoja is a Swahili word meaning 'Unity.' This EP is a musical reflection on togetherness, cultural memory, and the healing force of collective rhythm.
